Performance Reviews Help
About Performance Reviews

Performance reviews help track your progress, set goals, and identify areas for development.

Review Types
  • Annual Reviews: Comprehensive yearly evaluation
  • Quarterly Check-ins: Progress monitoring and goal adjustment
  • 30/60/90 Day Reviews: Onboarding and probation evaluations
  • 360-Degree Feedback: Peer and stakeholder perspectives
Rating System
  • 5 Stars: Exceptional performance, exceeds all expectations
  • 4 Stars: Exceeds expectations, strong performance
  • 3 Stars: Meets expectations, solid performance
  • 2 Stars: Needs improvement, below expectations
  • 1 Star: Unsatisfactory performance
Tips for Success
  • Keep a running list of accomplishments throughout the year
  • Request feedback regularly, not just during reviews
  • Set SMART goals with your manager
  • Be prepared with specific examples and metrics
  • View feedback as an opportunity for growth
